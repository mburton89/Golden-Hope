       Long ago, when the tree and the fruit were accessible to all, a small town developed on a hill across the valley from the tree. One of their traditions included using the judgement of the 'Eight Golden Judges' of the tree to determine whether or not a member of their community was eligible for adulthood. 
	One such candidate was a young man named Ahriman. He was becoming a man in his village, and so they sent him to the guardians of the tree, hoping the guardians may even allow him to take one of the fruits from the tree. None doubted that the guardians would judge Ahriman to be good and true...
